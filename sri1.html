About
Tulip garden festival is arranged in this beautiful place every year by the Horticultural department of Kashmir. In this festival, tulips are imported from Holland solely for this purpose. In 2014, you could find just 70 different varieties of tulips. This present year, there are even more number of varieties of tulips been planted. For sure, you will have a mesmerising time with your family at this place, which is rich in cultural and natural beauty. The scenic beauty is one of the best attractions of the place. You can enjoy the view of vibrant colours of the flowers and the dancing of these tulips to the slow breeze. Kashmiri folk dance, music and handicrafts are also a part of this festival. You can also savour the most famous and local dishes of Kashmir known as Rogan Josh and Phirni. Enjoy your day to the fullest and click some of the best pictures in this place.
