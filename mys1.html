About
Learn some innovative skills and new painting styles by learning the marvellous techniques of Tanjore painting. This splendid and attractive painting style is a famous South Indian painting form. The highlighting features of Tanjore painting are its rich, flat and exuberant colours, its iconic compositions, delicate and intricate work, abundant use of gold foils and many more. By coming to the spectacular class, you will open the door for all together a new style of painting. Learn to paint beautiful images, flowers and figures by using several materials. You will learn methods like to sticking cloth and foils on the canvas board, preparation of canvas board, sketching, sticking of stones and many more. Adorn your home by learning the fabulous painting style, and create some wonderful works.
