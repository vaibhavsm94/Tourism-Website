AboutKashmir is popular around the globe for the wonderful Shikara ride in the beautiful Dal Lake at Srinagar. If you are visiting this heavenly place, Shikara ride is must for you to make your trip perfect. Shikara ride, which is the centre of attraction to the tourists in Srinagar, is basically a boat ride. The small and beautiful boat of this area is known as Shikara. You will be able to explore the mesmerizing beauty of Kashmir and Dal Lake during this boat ride. This one hour of Shikara ride will be an eye pleaser, as you will get a chance to see the Golden Lake, Open Lake, Floating Market, Floating Garden, Vegetable Garden, Meena Bazaar and many more, along with the beautiful Nehru Park. Through this exhilarating Shikara ride, you will be able to observe the social life of Srinagar, along with the amazing scenic beauty of Kashmir.
